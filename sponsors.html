MTERMS 2026

The Malaysian Tissue Engineering and Regenerative Medicine Scientific Meeting 2026 (MTERMS 2026) is a premier biennial scientific conference organized by the Tissue Engineering and Regenerative Medicine Society of Malaysia (TESMA). The conference serves as a dynamic platform for advancing research, innovation, and collaboration in tissue engineering, regenerative medicine, cell and gene therapy, biomaterials, and related translational sciences.

MTERMS 2026 is expected to attract local and international scientists, clinicians, academicians, industry leaders, policy makers, and postgraduate students, providing sponsors with a unique opportunity to engage with a highly specialized and influential audience.

Why Sponsor MTERMS 2026?

By partnering with MTERMS 2026, your organization will gain:

  • High-visibility branding among a focused scientific and clinical community
  • Direct engagement with researchers, and emerging talent
  • Opportunities to showcase products, technologies, and services relevant to regenerative medicine and biomedical sciences
  • Recognition as a supporter of scientific advancement and innovation in Malaysia and the region
  • Long-term networking and collaboration opportunities with academia, healthcare institutions, and industry partners

Who Should Sponsor?

Sponsorship opportunities are ideal for:

  • Biotechnology and biomedical companies
  • Pharmaceutical and medical device companies
  • Cell therapy, gene therapy, and biomaterials industries
  • Research equipment and laboratory solution providers
  • CROs, start-ups, and innovation-driven enterprises
  • Publishers, professional bodies, and scientific organizations

Sponsorship Opportunities

A range of customizable sponsorship and exhibition packages will be available, including but not limited to:

  • Conference sponsorship
  • Exhibition booths (physical/virtual, subject to conference format)
  • Sponsored symposia or sessions
  • Branding opportunities (conference materials, website, signage, digital platforms)
  • Delegate materials and promotional inserts

Detailed sponsorship packages and benefits are outlined in the MTERMS 2026 Sponsorship Prospectus .
