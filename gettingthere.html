Travel options

Choose the route that fits your arrival style

The venue is well suited to car and e-hailing access, while rail users can also connect via KL Sentral and KTM Komuter before completing the final segment by car or e-hailing.

Airport arrival

From KLIA / KLIA2

Fastest and most convenient for most delegates

Concorde Hotel Shah Alam’s official site states the venue is about a 45-minute drive from Kuala Lumpur International Airport, making direct car transfer the simplest choice after landing.

1
After arrival, choose Grab, airport taxi, or pre-arranged transfer for the most direct trip to the venue.
2
Set your destination to Concorde Hotel Shah Alam in your navigation or ride-hailing app.
3
If you prefer rail from the airport, KLIA Transit connects KLIA T2 to KL Sentral in 39 minutes, after which you can continue onward by rail and short e-hailing transfer.

By Car / E-Hailing

Best option within Shah Alam and the Klang Valley

For most local delegates, driving or using e-hailing will be the easiest way to reach the conference venue. It is also the most practical last-mile option if you are arriving from a rail station or another hotel.

1
Use Waze, Google Maps, or your e-hailing app and search for Concorde Hotel Shah Alam.
2
If you are staying in Shah Alam, i-City, Glenmarie, or Setia Alam, direct car transfer will usually be the least complicated route.
3
Allow extra travel buffer during morning peak traffic, especially if you need to arrive before opening sessions.

By Rail / Public Transport

Good for delegates connecting from KL Sentral

Official Klang Valley rail maps show the Shah Alam LRT line is still marked coming soon, while KTMB’s Komuter timetable lists both Shah Alam and Padang Jawa stations. A practical approach is rail into the Shah Alam area, then complete the final segment by e-hailing.

1
If arriving from KLIA by train, continue first to KL Sentral.
2
From there, plan your onward route using KTM Komuter or the official Rapid KL Journey Planner.
3
Once you reach the Shah Alam area, use a short Grab / taxi ride to the venue for the easiest final transfer.

3
Keep a small time buffer in case of weekday congestion around Shah Alam.
