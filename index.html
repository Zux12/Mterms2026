About MTERMS

The Malaysian Tissue Engineering and Regenerative Medicine Scientific Meeting (MTERMS) is a biennial conference organised by the Tissue Engineering and Regenerative Medicine Society Malaysia (TESMA). First held in 2004, MTERMS has grown into an international platform that brings together researchers, clinicians, industry partners, and government agencies to strengthen tissue engineering and regenerative medicine.

Welcoming Remarks

Dear Colleagues,

On behalf of the Organizing Committee, it is our great pleasure to welcome you to the Malaysian Tissue Engineering & Regenerative Medicine Symposium (MTERMS) 2026. This symposium brings together leading researchers, clinicians, industry partners, and innovators to advance the rapidly evolving field of tissue engineering and regenerative medicine.

The theme of MTERMS 2026 reflects our shared commitment to transforming healthcare through innovative biomaterials, regenerative technologies, and interdisciplinary collaboration.
