About MTERMS

The Malaysian Tissue Engineering and Regenerative Medicine Scientific Meeting (MTERMS) is a biennial conference organised by the Tissue Engineering and Regenerative Medicine Society Malaysia (TESMA). First held in 2004, MTERMS has grown into an international platform that brings together researchers, clinicians, industry partners, and government agencies to strengthen tissue engineering and regenerative medicine.

With the theme, ‚ÄúSustainability through collaboration: building resilient tissue engineering and regenerative medicine ecosystem,‚Äù 10th MTERMS 2026 continues this mission by bringing together multidisciplinary expertise across tissue engineering, biomaterials, cell and gene-based therapies.

Conference Theme

FROM DISCOVERY TO THERAPY:
ADVANCES IN TISSUE ENGINEERING AND REGENERATIVE MEDICINE

Advancing tissue engineering and regenerative medicine requires a seamless pathway from fundamental discovery to safe, effective clinical application.

This theme highlights how breakthroughs in biomaterials, stem cells, bioprinting, and translational science are being transformed into real-world therapies. We emphasize interdisciplinary collaboration, scalable manufacturing, regulatory readiness, and industry‚Äìacademia partnerships that accelerate innovation from bench to bedside.
