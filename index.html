About MTERMS

10th Malaysian Tissue Engineering and Regenerative Medicine Scientific Meeting (MTERMS) is a biennial event organized by TESMA. The first was held in year 2004. The aim of the conference is to be a platform for all local researchers, industrial players and government agencies to work together and chart the way forward for regenerative medicine research & therapy in Malaysia.

  • Keynotes + plenaries on TERM science, clinical translation & manufacturing
  • Industry & standards sessions (GMP, ISO, regulatory updates)
  • Young investigator & poster awards
  • Networking with academia, hospitals, startups & policymakers

Conference Theme

FROM DISCOVERY TO THERAPY: ADVANCES IN TISSUE ENGINEERING AND REGENERATIVE MEDICINE

Advancing tissue engineering and regenerative medicine requires a seamless pathway from fundamental discovery to safe, effective clinical application. This theme highlights how breakthroughs in biomaterials, stem cells, bioprinting, and translational science are being transformed into real-world therapies. We emphasize interdisciplinary collaboration, scalable manufacturing, regulatory readiness, and industry‚Äìacademia partnerships that accelerate innovation from bench to bedside.

Biomaterials & scaffolds Stem cells & cellular therapies 3D bioprinting & biofabrication Translational research Clinical trials & therapy validation Regulatory & commercialization pathways
